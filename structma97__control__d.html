◆ ordering

int ma97_control_d::ordering

Control scaling algorithm used:

  • 0 - user supplied order (order absent=identity)
  • 1 - AMD
  • 2 - MD (as in MA27)
  • 3 - METIS nested dissection
  • 4 - MA47
  • 5 - Automatic choice between 1 and 3
